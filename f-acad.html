Academic Facilities of GACV

Library

The Library of was established in 1956, and is situated in the Government Ayurved College - Vadodara. Library having more than 10,000 books of different subjects such as Rachna Sharir, Shamita, Rog Nidan, Shalakya, Kaya Chikitsa, Kaumarabhritya, Swasthvritta, Panchkarma, Dravaguna, Ras Shastra, Shalyatantra, SRPT, Kriya Sharir and Agadtantra and nearly 300 journals/ periodicals/ magazines. Library is complemented with a 14 of Departmental Libraries. The library a created learning environment by providing accession of library materials , library resources, Reading facility, Wi-Fi facility. All the library facilities are open to all the members of the College and Hospital. The primary objective of the library is to support the college in creating a research-based learning space. The library remains open on all days, all through the year from 9.00 am to 5.00 pm, except on Sundays and public holidays declared by the institution.

Herbal Garden

A botanical garden conserved with medicinal plants of various habitats is attached to this department covering 4000 sq. feet land area approximately. Botanical garden is enriched with 1372 total species which includes 225 climbers, 872 herbs, 219 tree and 56 shrubs.
