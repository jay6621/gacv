AboutGovernment Ayurved College, Vadodara

About College

Government Ayurved College, Vadodara (GACV) is an eminent institute which provides Teaching and consultancy in the field of Ayurveda since 1966. It is located in the centre of the cultural city Vadodara.

Few details about GACV :

This Institute is recognised by CCIM, New Delhi and affiliated with Gujarat Ayurved University, Jamnagar. Initially the institute was offering four years diploma course, D.S.A.C. (Diploma in Shuddha Ayurved Course) with intake capacity of 30 seats under the leadership of Vaidya Khandaparshu Chhatrasinhji Barot. Currently, the college is running Undergraduate (B.A.M.S.) and Postgraduate (M.D.) courses, with intake capacity of 75 UG and 13 PG students respectively.

The college has an attached hospital having capacity of 100 beds which provides healthcare services through 12 different special OPDs through clinical specialists in different branches.

And few more :

College has well-developed Departments, Classrooms, Library, Museums, Seminar Hall, Hostels and Herbal Garden. Hospital provides high-quality patient care by qualified and experienced clinicians with Laboratories, Physiotherapy unit, Wellness centre, Pharmacy, Homeopathy Unit along with trained Nursing staff.

Welcome !

Vadodara city is famed as an educational hub with many institutions of national and international repute, required Ayurved institution which has been fulfilled with the establishment of this institution. Started in 1956 by Govt. of Gujarat to serve Ayurveda knowledge with an intake of 30 students, it now has been increased to 75 (60 +15) in BAMS course and 13 in M.D. course of two subjects i.e. Rasashastra and Bhaishajya Kalpana and Dravyaguna from year 2020.

Affiliation & Administration

This institute is administered under office of Director AYUSH, Department of Health and Family Welfare, Govt. Of Gujarat and affiliated to Gujarat Ayurved University, Jamnagar. Students are selected merely on the basis of merit through a competitive exam. Our main motto is to provide excellent teaching and learning methodology through various pedagogical approaches to the Ayurved students. So, this institute becomes a preferred institution amongst undergraduate and postgraduate students for Ayurved education who are seeking admissions through All India Quota.
